Resume Workshop

A workshop on Resume Building was conducted in association with the Rethink Foundation. Through the workshop, it was intended to introduceparticipants to the nuances of resume etiquette. The day long programmeworked towards instilling good resume writing skills, with a focus on exploring job descriptions for each persons dream job while still in college, so as tocultivate the relevant industry skills and create resumes that represent them better.

The resume workshop provides delegates with a detailed step by step processto create an impressive resume. A reputed entrepreneur will handle this session, and around 35 participants can attend this 3 hour workshop. Exploringwhat goes into making a great resume also motivates students to work towards building the necessary skills and experiences that can land them theirdream job, Online services will be provided for individual resume critique for the attendees.

Outcome

• At least 65% of participants should have made a sample resume.
• All delegates should be aware of the inevitable contents of a job resume.
